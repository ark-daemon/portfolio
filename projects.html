Interactive Engine · Glossary / to search
  • Support & ResistancePrice Action

    Price levels where an asset historically reverses or pauses. Support is a floor where buying pressure exceeds selling; resistance is a ceiling where selling pressure exceeds buying.

  • Risk/Reward RatioRisk Management

    The ratio of potential profit to potential loss on a trade. A 1:3 RR means risking $1 to target $3 in gain, a core filter before entering any position.

  • Liquidity SweepMarket Structure

    A sharp price move that clears clustered stop-loss orders above a high or below a low before reversing in the opposite direction.

  • HTF BiasMulti-Timeframe

    The directional trend on a higher timeframe used to filter lower-timeframe entries.

  • Funding RateDerivatives

    A periodic payment between longs and shorts on perpetual futures to tether price to spot.

  • Order BlockSmart Money

    The last opposing candle before a significant impulsive move, often marking institutional interest.
